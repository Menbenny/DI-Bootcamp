Bobby Fischer

Bobby Fischer

Bobby Fischer was an American chess grandmaster and former world chess champion. He is considered to be one of the greatest chess players of all time, and he is the only player to have won the world championship three times. Fischer was a controversial figure, and he is known for his eccentric behavior and his outspoken views on chess and politics.
